　　　　from the inside out　　由内而外，从里到外，彻底　　This phrase refers to all the qualities of something from its inside to outside； or refers to thoroughness and comprehensiveness。　　这个短语指的是某事物从内在到外在的全部特质；或指彻底、全面。　　He was changed from the inside out。　　他由内而外地改变了。　　You really need to clean your house from the inside out。　　你真的应该把你房子从里到外打扫一遍了。　　来源：每日学英语微信公众账号　　责任编辑：陈熙
